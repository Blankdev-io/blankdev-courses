Common mistakes


  • When starting to dropship products, start small, don’t overflow your store with dozens of products.
  • Make research on products that you know people will want and are unique. If the average customer can just order it from Amazon or go to any shop and find it at the same price, it’s not worth dropshipping.
  • Find products that you know you can sell for at least 3 times their price. This is especially true for products that are really cheap (less than $10) as customers associate these prices to products that are “bad” or “low quality”.
  • Don’t market your product without having a Shopify website ready and optimized. A lot of people think they can fulfill orders directly through emails or DMs, this is basically a repellent for potential customers. If you want a great looking Shopify store, be sure to check the templates we have on sale!
  • An unoptimized Shopify store can lower your sales by up to 90%. Customers have a short attention span, if you don’t lay out information in an efficient way to increase CRO, visitors will leave your site.

Choosing a Product


The first step of starting an ecommerce store is deciding what you want to sell.
You need to research your target market and figure out which products are ideal to sell online and if there is a demand for them.
Here are some good ways to find a good product to sell:

Explore popular products online

One way to gauge demand is to see what people want. An easy way to do this is to check the popular listings on sites such as eBay, Etsy, Craigslist, and Amazon. For example, on eBay, you can head to ebay.com/trending which displays the most recently popular items on the site.

If you already have a product in mind and want information about consumer demand, you can visit explore.ebay.com, which lets you conduct trend searches for specific items. There are also third-party tools like WatchCount.com, which enable you to “discover which eBay items have attracted the most interest.”

In Amazon you can navigate to the site’s Best Sellers page to view the top-selling products. Items are organized by department. Etsy has a ‘popular right now’ section that allows you to find the most sought-after products.

Appeal to a Niche

A good strategy you can employ is researching products in demand that belong to a niche category. These products are often requested by passionate consumers who don’t mind spending a higher sum of money for a product they really want. Not to mention these kinds of customers remain loyal to a certain brand.

Even if you’ve found the product you want to make, there is always a chance to expand your business. For that reason, we advise you to always keep an open mind and continue to grow by constantly researching your target market and keeping an open mind.

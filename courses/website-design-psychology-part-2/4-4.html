The Importance of a Copy


Whether we are trying to encourage social sign-ups or the purchasing of a particular product, the copy associated with that call to action is crucial to conversion.

This need to write a copy that encourages conversion can lead to short-term thinking. A failure to keep this in check can result in exaggeration and verbose copy, which in turn leads to buyerâ€™s remorse and undermines conversion in the long term.

Try to avoid the mistake of writing large blocks of copy that nobody reads, and instead make great use of bullets to summarize key selling points.

Conclusion


We have looked at the psychology that dictates a user’s decision to act, and introduced some key concepts that will aid conversion on any merchant site. These concepts include:

Identifying customer desires and establishing the universal human needs that products can fulfill Considering the different criteria that create perceived value of products and brands Recognizing the social influences that friends, family, experts, or even celebrities have on potential customers Understanding a customers mental model and staying true to their expectations Avoiding choice paralysis by reducing the number of options that are presented to a customer and ensuring the decision-making process is efficient Considering the effects that aesthetics have on a user’s inherent bias, trust, and perceived value Understanding how smart defaults can promote cognitive ease and take the burden of choice off the user Recognizing the potential harm of using “dark patterns” to persuade users to make snap purchases that are often ill-considered If you’re interested in owning a well crafted website that takes into consideration modern standards to increase sales, feel free to check out template store [LINK].

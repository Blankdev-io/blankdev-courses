Editing and Managing Products


The Shopify Admin has some key features to make managing and editing products easier for merchants:

  • Product filtering
  • Exporting and editing product CSVs
  • Bulk editor

Gift cards

You may want to offer gift cards to your customers, online or in store. Shoppers can buy gift cards to use as payment for future purchases or you can issue free gift cards to customers as an incentive or part of a reward program.

Merchants need to be on the Shopify Plan or higher to sell gift cards in their store. Gift cards are listed under the products section of the store.

Collections

Organizing products by collections intuitively makes it easier for customers to find what they need and contributes to higher conversion rates. Products can be grouped into collections so customers can shop by category, such as women, brand, size, season, or sale.

There are two types of collections:

  • Manual: You add the products using the browse function on the collection page. The collection always contains the same products unless you add or remove them.
  • Automated: You can define collection conditions and the system will automatically include matching products, including any new products that you add that meet the collection’s conditions.

Once you save a collection as manual or automated, this cannot be edited. If you decide to switch from one method to the other, you’ll need to re-create the collection from scratch. Consider how you will organize your navigation when mapping collections.

Discounts

Offering discounts can be a powerful marketing strategy for online stores and should be a part of a larger marketing strategy.

You can set up four types of discounts from the admin, or use the PriceRule API:

  • Monetary
  • Percentage
  • Free shipping
  • Buy X get Y

Once you’ve chosen the type of discount, the following conditions will be available:

  • Minimum purchase amount
  • Products/Collection discount applies to
  • Customer eligibility
  • Usage limits
  • Options for shipping discounts, such as choosing a country and excluding shipping rates over a certain amount

You can also schedule start and end dates for discounts and promote them with shareable links, in notifications and email marketing.

When a manual discount code is created, it can be promoted by generating a shareable link in the discounts page. Shareable discount links increase the likelihood of a customer landing on the store to take advantage of savings.

You can choose to have the link redirect the customer to the homepage, the collection, or the product page associated with that discount. When the customer clicks the link, the discount will be automatically applied to checkout.

Notification Settings


Shopify provides automated notifications to aid in the automation of merchantâ€™s fulfillment process. Notifications are triggered automatically to save time and ensure customers are responded to promptly. In notifications settings, you can view, stylize, and customize messages that will be sent to customers, fulfillment services, and team members when trigger conditions are met after an order is paid.

Notification are broken down into the following groups:

  • Orders
  • Shipping
  • Customer
  • Order notifications (fulfillment and staff emails)
  • Webhooks

Orders and shipping

Notifications can be customized using Liquid variables and HTML for proactive customer service and additional marketing.

Most order, shipping, and customer account notifications are mandatory and sent to customers automatically. These can't be disabled.

When the merchant is processing an order, you can disable a few of the notifications for actions on that order:

  • Order canceled
  • Order refund
  • Shipping confirmation
  • Shipping update

Key considerations

  • Shopify Plus merchants can suppress shipping and order confirmation notifications with the help of Merchant Success Managers
  • Shipment out for delivery and shipment delivered notifications can be disabled with the help of Shopify Support
  • On the Basic Shopify Plan, Shopify Plan, Advanced Shopify Plan, or Shopify Plus Plan, merchants can edit the schedule of automated abandoned checkout notifications
  • Staff members can receive new order notifications by email, through the Shopify Mobile App, through desktop notifications, or through webhooks

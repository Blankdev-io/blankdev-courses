Orders

Order fulfillment workflows vary from store to store. Before you fulfill an order, ensure you explore the orders page to get a good grasp of what data to pay attention to on the order summary page. They should understand how to:

  • Read fraud analysis to make proactive steps to avoid chargebacks
  • Update customer information
  • Email a customer from the order page
  • Analyze conversion tracking
  • Add notes to customers
  • Analyze, add to, and perform actions from the timeline
  • Tag orders

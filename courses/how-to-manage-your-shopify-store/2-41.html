Interest stage

Brand awareness efforts need to lead customers through the funnel to a user experience that builds trust and answers questions they may have about the products in a store.

Product page optimization

At the interest stage, social media content should link to specific product pages instead of the homepage. As a landing page, product pages should be able to stand on their own with details, links and social proof that increase trust. You can optimize a product page by doing the following:

  • Adding product reviews
  • Embedding videos from the brand’s YouTube channel
  • Creating a shoppable Instagram gallery using hashtags for user generated photos
  • Linking an article from your blog or your FAQ page in the product description

Content marketing

The right, consistent content can drive sales, especially when it provides information that helps real people solve a problem. Good content is an opportunity for the merchant to add value in a creative piece and show how their products can make their audience’s lives better.

Lead generation

When visitors don’t convert on the first visit, strategic lead generation gives merchants the opportunity to bring visitors back to their store through email marketing. In Shopify, merchants can collect emails from visitors who haven’t purchased in their online store in three ways:

  • Abandoned checkouts
  • Embedded newsletter sign-ups
  • Pop-up forms
  • Abandoned checkouts

When a prospective customer enters their contact details in the first stage of checkout then leaves the online store without purchasing, an abandoned checkout is automatically created in the orders section of the store. Merchants can send a cart recovery email from the Shopify Admin.

Newsletter sign-ups


Shopify themes include a newsletter sign-up as a section on the homepage. As a partner, you can also add opt-in forms to blog articles, static pages, product pages, and collection page theme templates to increase the probability of buy-in.

Pop-up forms

Pop-up forms are an effective opt-in vehicle for ecommerce stores. They work best when created with an incentive or special offer. Merchants can offer immediate discounts, exclusive content or digital products, and membership that promises long term savings. Some Shopify themes have built in pop-up form capabilities allowing merchants to control triggers, copy, and images. You can also find pop-up apps in the Shopify App Store that offer a range of styles and use cases.

Remarketing

Visitors may not opt-in to any of the list building prompts or add a product to their cart, but merchants can still show targeted ads to those visitors while they browse other websites. This is called remarketing.

In Shopify, you can connect the online store with advertising platforms for remarketing. Run Facebook Dynamic Ads with Kit, set up Google Remarketing, or use other third-party remarketing apps.

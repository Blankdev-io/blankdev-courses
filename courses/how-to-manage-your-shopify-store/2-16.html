The Product Editor Page


There is a lot of information on a product's details page, and it's worth your time to get to know it. Not all fields are mandatory or apply to all merchants. For example, a merchant would only need to add a barcode if they were also selling in person or using a sales channel like Google Shopping or Amazon.

You can use the product import feature or the Shopify API to efficiently upload product catalogs if switching from another platform. Many of the settings on the product editor page affect configuration of other areas like shipping, collections, and taxes and fulfillment, which we’ll cover in later chapters. Ensure you’re paying close attention to these settings:

  • Weight
  • Requires shipping
  • Charge taxes
  • Fulfillment service
  • Organization

While saving a product only requires entering a title and price, the additional product details give customers the key details they need to make purchasing decisions.

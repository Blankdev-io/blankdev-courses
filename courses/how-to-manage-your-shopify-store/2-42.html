Evaluation stage

At the evaluation stage of the funnel, customers have all the information provided about the brand and its products, and are deciding if they will make a purchase. Below are some marketing techniques to target customers in this stage.

Live chat

For online stores, offering immediate and ongoing customer service can turn skeptics into buyers and give interested shoppers the answers they need to complete a purchase. Live chat is a good way to personalize the experience on the online store.

Deciding what kind of chat service a store will offer depends greatly on the business’ human resources. For stores with limited staff, you can recommend chat apps that can allow merchants to chat from mobile devices, send an email when questions come in and the merchant’s status is offline, or answer preset frequently asked questions. View chat apps in the Shopify App Store.

Promotions

Sales and promotions are an effective way to convert hesitant shoppers, often by discounting prices. From the Shopify Admin, merchants can set sales prices on products that get a lot of traffic but aren’t being bought. Merchants can also use discount codes for timed promotions or for specific segments of an email list.

Discounting products regularly doesn’t work for every ecommerce business. Depending on your business model, consider these other methods of promotion that can be managed from the Shopify Admin:

  • Offer free shipping over a certain order value
  • Create a sense of urgency and scarcity with purchase countdown timers and low stock notifications
  • Upsell products in the cart
  • Offer bundles

Learn from Google Analytics

Google Analytics can give insights on where to focus attention on optimizing a store and which promotions are working. Your analysis can start from where visitors spend the most time in the online store and where they drop off.

The audience insights can tell you if the store would benefit from localization or internationalization to help you refine your client’s advertising spend. If the store sees a high drop off at the second stage of checkout, maybe you need to evaluate shipping costs. This method is great for identifying where to optimize the store.

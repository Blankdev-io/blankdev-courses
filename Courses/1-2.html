Should you Dropship or Hold Stock?


The answer to this question mostly comes down to one thing: your budget.

Holding stock has its advantages:

  • Since you ship the product yourself you guarantee it goes out in time.
  • You physically own the object, and you can answer quickly to faulty products by shipping another one to the customer.

But holding stock also has its disadvantages:

  • Requires a big initial investment.
  • If you buy too much of a product and it doesn’t sell, you won’t get that investment back.
  • You will need space to store your stock.
  • You will need to ship out the product yourself.

Now for the advantages of Dropshipping:

  • It has a low initial investment.
  • You don’t have to buy a product in advance.
  • You don’t have to allocate space to store your products.
  • The product is shipped by your supplier.

And the disadvantages of Dropshipping:

  • If you source your products from China, it’s probable those products take more than usual to arrive at their destination. There’s also a chance of them being faulty.
  • You have to allocate more time to answering messages from customers for delayed shipments or faulty products.
